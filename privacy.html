Privacy Policy

Big Wonders Kids apps — effective July 13, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all Big Wonders Kids apps, including Big Wonders: Space Play (the "Apps"), created by Jonathan Leithead. Our Apps are made for young children, and we take that responsibility seriously. The short version: we collect nothing.

No Data Collection

Our Apps do not collect, store, or share any personal information or any other data — from children or adults. Specifically, the Apps contain:

  • No data collection or transmission of any kind
  • No third-party analytics or tracking
  • No advertising of any kind
  • No in-app purchases
  • No user accounts or registration
  • No access to the microphone, camera, contacts, or location

Everything in the Apps runs entirely on your device. Settings (like sound preferences) are stored only on your device and never leave it.

External Links

The Apps contain a single link to our YouTube channel. This link is placed behind a parental gate (a press-and-hold control designed for adults) and opens outside the App in the YouTube app or browser. YouTube's own privacy policy applies there. Children cannot reach this link without passing the parental gate.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Because our Apps collect no data whatsoever, no personal information from children under 13 (or any user) is collected, used, or disclosed, in accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide, including the GDPR-K.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the Apps work in a way that affects privacy, we will update this policy and the "effective" date above before the change takes effect.
